STM (Short-Term Memory) — Annual Budget Context:

This is dynamic, task-specific, and resets each budgeting cycle
Contains:
Current fiscal year constraints (e.g., ₹ budget ceiling)
Department-wise allocations under discussion
Live proposals and drafts
Scenario comparisons (optimistic / baseline / austerity)
Current inflation, GDP assumptions for THIS year
Temporary policy changes
Negotiation inputs between ministries

LTM (Long-Term Memory) — Institutional Knowledge:

This is persistent across years
Contains:
Historical budget allocations (last 10–20 years)
Policy frameworks (e.g., FRBM Act, fiscal deficit rules)
Sector growth patterns
Infrastructure lifecycle costs
Strategic national priorities
Demographic projections
International commitments (SDGs, defence treaties)


A. Commute / Transport Budgeting:

LTM (knowledge base):

Metro projects take 8–12 years
Urban congestion rising 6% yearly
Historical ROI of rail vs road investment
Past infrastructure backlog in cities

STM (this year plan):

Allocate ₹X for metro expansion in Bengaluru
Fuel subsidy adjustment for FY 2026–27
EV charging station rollout target this year


 B. Defence Budgeting
LTM:
Minimum deterrence doctrine
10-year procurement cycles
Border threat history trends
Equipment aging curves (fighter jets, naval fleets)

STM
This year’s procurement (drones, aircraft, missiles)
Emergency border deployment funding
Salary + pension adjustments for forces

C. Agriculture Budgeting

LTM
Crop yield trends over decades
Monsoon dependency patterns
MSP (Minimum Support Price) history
Soil degradation statistics

STM
Fertilizer subsidy for this year
Drought relief allocation
Farmer income support scheme update
Crop insurance payout for current season

D. Government (General Fiscal Budget)

LTM
Fiscal deficit rules (FRBM limits)
Tax elasticity trends
Welfare scheme effectiveness history
Demographic shifts (aging population, urbanization)

STM
Tax slab changes for current year
Welfare scheme budget allocation
Short-term stimulus package
Emergency disaster funds
