Request for Proposal — Supply Chain Visibility Platform
=======================================================

1. Client / Organisation
  Pied Piper Logistics

2. RFP Reference Number
  RFP-2026-LOG-259

3. Solution Domain
  Logistics / Supply Chain

4. Region
  Global

5. Submission Deadline
  03 Sep 2026

6. Background
  Pied Piper Logistics seeks a real-time supply chain visibility platform to track shipments end-to-end, predict delays, and optimise routing across global lanes.

7. Functional Requirements
  - Provide end-to-end shipment tracking across ocean, air, and road.
  - Deliver predictive ETA and delay alerts.
  - Integrate with carrier APIs and EDI feeds.
  - Offer a control-tower dashboard with exception management.
  - Provide route and cost optimisation recommendations.

8. Non-Functional Requirements
  - Ingest tracking events from 200 carriers in near real time.
  - Dashboard refresh under 5 seconds.
  - Availability of 99.9%.

9. Compliance & Security Requirements
  - GDPR compliance for any personal data in shipping records.
  - SOC 2 Type II certified.
  - Customs and trade-compliance data handling.

10. Commercial / Pricing Line Items
  - Visibility platform subscription (per shipment, 500000 shipments/yr)
  - Carrier integration package (per carrier, 200 carriers)
  - Implementation services (labor, estimated 1800 hours)
  - Support & SLA (annual)

11. Evaluation Criteria
  - Visibility & prediction 40%
  - Integration breadth 25%
  - Price 20%
  - Compliance 15%

12. Submission Instructions
  - Responses must be submitted in PDF or DOCX format by the submission deadline.
  - Vendors must address every functional and compliance requirement explicitly.
  - Pricing must be itemised and valid for 90 days.
