Request for Proposal — 5G Private Network Deployment
====================================================

1. Client / Organisation
  Hooli Telecom

2. RFP Reference Number
  RFP-2026-TEL-410

3. Solution Domain
  Telecommunications

4. Region
  Asia Pacific

5. Submission Deadline
  30 Aug 2026

6. Background
  Hooli Telecom seeks a vendor to design and deploy a private 5G network for an industrial campus, enabling low-latency connectivity for automation and AGVs.

7. Functional Requirements
  - Design and deploy a private 5G standalone (SA) network across a 2 sq km campus.
  - Provide 30 small-cell radio units with full indoor and outdoor coverage.
  - Deliver network slicing for critical and best-effort traffic.
  - Integrate with the campus core and existing Wi-Fi.
  - Provide a network management and monitoring portal.

8. Non-Functional Requirements
  - End-to-end latency under 10 ms for critical slices.
  - 99.99% network availability.
  - Support 5,000 connected devices.

9. Compliance & Security Requirements
  - Comply with national telecom spectrum regulations.
  - Adhere to 3GPP Release 16 standards.
  - Data residency within the country.

10. Commercial / Pricing Line Items
  - 5G core & RAN equipment (per site)
  - Small-cell radio units (per unit, 30 units)
  - Deployment & integration (labor, estimated 2500 hours)
  - Managed network operations (per year)

11. Evaluation Criteria
  - Network design 40%
  - Performance & SLAs 25%
  - Price 20%
  - Compliance 15%

12. Submission Instructions
  - Responses must be submitted in PDF or DOCX format by the submission deadline.
  - Vendors must address every functional and compliance requirement explicitly.
  - Pricing must be itemised and valid for 90 days.
